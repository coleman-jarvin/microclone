microclone - backpropagation from scratch

--- 
A minimal autograd engine built from scratch with backpropagation and jupyter notebook visualization.


--- | Teaching a computer how to correct it's mistakes, mathematically | --- 

- eng.py is the autograd engine keeping track of operations and labeling mistakes as it computes

- nn.py is the neural network built from scratch taking the mathematical ops from eng.py and creating neurons, neuronal layers and a multilayer perceptron which then used the operations to perform back propagation

- demo.ipynb is the visualization of the process in a jupyter notebook :)
